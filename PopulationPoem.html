POPULATION

India, of all the existing nations,

Has the second highest population.

But here is a message clear and loud,

This is not a thing to be proud.

Surplus population leads to overconsumption,

Fulfilment of our needs leads to nature’s exploitation.

The forests are being converted to lands,

Soon the nature will be reduced to sands.

Prevention is the immediate need,

Families don’t have food to feed.

A small family of three,

Can also live in love and glee.

The day is not so far,

When we cross China’s mark.

And here’s a message clear and loud,

This is not a thing to be proud.~Devyanshi Sarikwal
